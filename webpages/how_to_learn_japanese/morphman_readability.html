Manga

Study Japanese with your favourite manga. You can download the anki deck for Yotsuba Volume 1&2 for free here.

Here's what's included with each download:

  • The anki deck always include japanese sentence and the screenshot of the panel with the sentence. It can also include the english translation.
  • The study plan will make sure you learn words by appearance (by chapters) if you use it with Morphman. See the tutorial for more details.
  • For those who don't use Anki, you can also find a frequency list for all the words that appear in the manga.

Each deck has the Japanese sentence and a screenshot of the sentence. The "Id field" lets you know the line is from.

The back card will automatically deconstruct the sentence. You can change the presentation by editing the card.

The study plan is an archive with individual text files for each chapters.

Use the study plan with Morphman to study sentences from a chapter until you reached the target you set for your self and move on to the next chapter.

The Frequency List will make sure you focus on the most important words.
