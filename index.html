I present to you a comprehensive tutorial on different methods to learn Japanese and provide you with resources to achieve your goals.

Method

Choose how to learn

You will be able to learn Japanese using your favourite Anime, Manga, Movie, Video Game or a TV show.

Use the right tools

I center my studies around Anki and Morphman so most of my resources focus on that. I've created some premade files, so you can use them even if you've never studied with Anki or Morphman before.

Key Features

Word count

Keep track of how many words you already know.

Understanding

Know exactly what percentage of a title you understand, helping you finding suitable material for you.

Morphman

You will learn to use Morphman plugin to prioritize the most important words.

Comprehension

You can set the exact percentage of comprehension you want for your next study.

Filtering

Use a frequency list to make sure you still focus on the most frequent words in Japanese in general and not just words used in a specific show.

Study Plan

The study plan will make sure to order the words you're studying by the episode they appear in.
